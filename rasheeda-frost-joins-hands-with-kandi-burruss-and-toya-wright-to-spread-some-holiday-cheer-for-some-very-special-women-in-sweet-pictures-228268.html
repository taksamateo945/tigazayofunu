Rasheeda Frost Joins Hands With Kandi Burruss And Toya Wright To Spread Some Holiday Cheer For Some

May 2024 · 2 minute read

All in the spirit of giving. Kandi Burruss and her good friends and soon-to-be business partners — Rasheeda Frost and Toya Wright — united to give back to the community and spread cheers.

The ladies gathered a group of mothers and had surprise events in their honor.

The moms and their children were showered with gifts and other goodies that will help them enjoy the holiday season.

Both Toya an Kandi shared tons of photos where their guests were having fun opening presents with their children.

https://www.instagram.com/p/Brxz-agjFGo/

Kandi had this to say about the heartwarming gathering: “Over the weekend me and my girls @rasheedadabosschick @toyawright made Christmas dreams come true for 10 families with the Beautiful Bosses Toy Workshop. We surprised 10 moms with everything on their children’s wishlist, complete with brunch, Santa, gift wrapping and more. Special thanks to @djtarxan @chef_petey @thegatheringspots @jasminecrowe and all of our amazing volunteers! Wishing everyone a Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays! #BeautifulBosses #GiveBack.”

Toya also penned a sweet message about the ladies at the special event: “My girls and I came together to make sure these beautiful mothers have an amazing Christmas. We picked a few families and bought everything on their children’s Christmas lists. They had no idea that we were doing this. They thought they were meeting with someone to see about something else entirely. I enjoyed every moment with these ladies…it’s such a blessing when you are able to bless others. I know what’s it’s like to not have so I love giving when I’m able. #BBC #giveback #christmas2018.”

One fan told the ladies that they were inspirational and praised them for their good deeds: “That’s a beautiful thing! God bless you ladies for blessing someone who needed help.”

This supporter had the following to say about the charity event: “That is so awesome what you guys have done!!! May God bless you all!!!”

This third person wrote: “Thank you, beautiful ladies, for allowing me and my husband to help in serving the community and these beautiful women! It was an excellent experience!”
