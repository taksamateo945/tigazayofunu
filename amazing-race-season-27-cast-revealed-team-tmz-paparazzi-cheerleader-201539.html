Amazing Race Season 27 Cast Revealed: Team TMZ, Paparazzi, Cheerleader

April 2024 · 2 minute read

This race will definitely be amazing. The Season 27 cast of The Amazing Race was revealed on Thursday, Sept. 3, and 11 teams will face off against each other, including a set of TMZ employees and two members of the paparazzi.
