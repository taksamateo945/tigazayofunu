Tory Lanez Reportedly Took His Shot At One Of Futures Baby Mommas

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Earlier this year, Future said that Eliza Reign, a woman he had a child with and is now required to pay child support to, “belonged to the streets.” Hot New Hip Hop reported today that Tory Lanez appears to disagree with that idea.

The Shade Room claims Eliza Reign has been hanging around Tory Lanez. The Toronto artist reportedly transformed his kitchen into a nightclub including both flashy lights and women as well.

One of the girls there was Eliza Reign, whom the outlet says likely had to get a babysitter for the night to take care of her 1-year-old daughter. Thus far, it’s not clear if it really was Eliza in the video, but Hot New Hip Hop says they have tattoos in the same spots.

Put simply, because of the woman’s similarity to Eliza, fans are convinced that it really is her in the video. If that is the case, Lanez has to be careful, because Eliza has shown she is relentless when it comes to getting what she thinks she deserves.

Hot New Hip Hop says that Eliza has been taking Future to court for a $53,000 per month child support deal. Future’s romantic life has been in the headlines a number of times before, especially for his many baby mommas, which even Wendy Williams has addressed.

Tory, on the other hand, was in the headlines yesterday when fans noticed that he liked a tweet dissing Nicki Minaj. As it was previously reported, fans online saw that the rapper had liked a tweet which suggested Cardi B was a superior artist.

Nicki Minaj and Tekashi 6ix9ine’s new song together, “TROLLZ,” fell from the number one position catastrophically this year. According to Hot New Hip Hop, it dropped from #1 all the way down to #34 in the span of just a week.
