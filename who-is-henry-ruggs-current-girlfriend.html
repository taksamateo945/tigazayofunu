Who is Henry Ruggs' Current Girlfriend

May 2024 · 5 minute read

American football wide receiver Henry Ruggs has been dating his current girlfriend, Rudy Washington since their high school days. While they are not married, they are parents to a daughter named Kenzli Re’Nai. She is currently 3 years old as she was born on the 7th of May 2020. 

Henry Ruggs’ NFL career which is the primary source of his popularity has rubbed off on Rudy Washington who many regards as his wife. Their relationship status came into question in November 2021 when some platforms reported that the football player was arguing with Rudy when he rear-ended another car, killing Tina Tintor and her dog in Las Vegas. While it was claimed in some quarters that Rudy is his wife, we can confirm that Henry Ruggs has never been married.

Who Is Henry Rugg’s Girlfriend?

Henry Rugg’s girlfriend is Rudy Washington who was named Kiara Je’Nai Kilgo-Washington at birth. According to reports, she was born in Montgomery, Alabama, where she spent most of her early life. She was reportedly born sometime in November 1998. This means she is 25 years old at the moment and about a year older than her famous boyfriend.

It is no secret that Henry James Ruggs III, as he was named at birth, was born on the 24th of January 1999. So he is 24 years old right now. Like his girlfriend, Rudy Washington, he was also born and raised in Montgomery, Alabama. He was raised alongside his younger brother Kevontae Ruggs by their parents, Nataki and Henry Ruggs Jr. From what we gathered, he comes from a sporting family. His mother Nataki was a high-school track champion and his younger brother, Kevontae Ruggs, is a former college football player.

All of such information about his girlfriend’s family background is yet to be uncovered. The family she was born to, who her parents are, and the number of siblings she has is not known to the public. The reason for this is not far-fetched. Even though Rudy Washington has a lot going for her, she only gained public attention through her relationship with Henry Ruggs who the Las Vegas Raiders selected with the 12th overall pick at the 2020 NFL Draft.

Henry Ruggs and Rudy Washington Have Been Dating Since High School

Henry Ruggs and Rudy Washington’s relationship started during their teenage years. Given that, you would agree that it is remarkable that they are still together. It is not clear how and when the two met, just as the circumstances surrounding the beginning of the relationship remain unknown to the public. However, reports have it that the two began dating during their high school days.

Because of the fact that the two started their relationship while they were in high school, some reports have suggested that they attended the same high school. Based on the facts available, this is not true. According to records, Henry Ruggs attended  Robert E. Lee High School, a public high school in Montgomery, Alabama, whereas Rudy Washington attended Park Crossing High School which is also a public high school in Montgomery, Alabama.

So while they didn’t attend the same high school, they were brought up in the same area which explains how they have been in each other’s life since their teenage years. Given that they have been together for many years, it is surprising that they were able to keep their relationship away from the public.

Their Relationship Was Thrown into the Limelight After Ruggs’ Accident in November 2021

A lot of people weren’t aware who the NFL wide receiver was dating until his fatal car crash on the 2nd of November 2021. It was reported that Henry Ruggs rear-ended another car at high speed in Las Vegas. The accident claimed the life of 23-year-old Tina Tintor and her dog. As such, the NFL star is currently facing charges of reckless driving and driving under the influence resulting in death.

Rudy Washington was with Ruggs in the car when the accident happened. According to some reports, she was arguing with the wide receiver when the crash happened. This gained her some measure of unwanted publicity even though the claims that she was arguing with Ruggs hadn’t been meaningfully confirmed. Rudy reportedly injured her waist in the accident but is said to have fully recovered.

The Couple are Parents to a Daughter Named Kenzli Re’Nai Ruggs

While Henry Ruggs and Rudy Washington are not a married couple, it hasn’t deterred them from starting a family. Although we can’t tell if it was something that was planned, we can confirm that the couple has a child. Ruggs and Washington welcomed their daughter, Kenzli Re’Nai Ruggs, on the 7th of May 2020. This means she is 3 years old right now.

Considering her age, it is only natural that much isn’t known about Kenzli right now. Pictures of her can be found all over her parents’ social media pages. Henry and Rudy are undeniably happy and proud to have her as part of their family.

What Does Henry Ruggs’s Girlfriend Do for a Living?

Like her famous boyfriend, Rudy Washington is also an athlete. While she is yet to make it to the professional stage as an athlete, it is said that she has been drawn to sports since she was a kid.

She reportedly played volleyball during her days in high school. Rudy was part of the Park Crossing High School volleyball team. After high school, she decided to further her education at Paine College. She also plays volleyball at the private and historically black Methodist college in Augusta, Georgia.

At the moment, it is not known if Henry Ruggs’ girlfriend intends to pursue a professional career as a volleyball player. But apart from that, she is a social media influencer and a vlogger with a YouTube channel. As a social media influencer, she has over 14 thousand followers on her Instagram page. She shares snippets of her private life with Henry Ruggs on the platform which she also uses for sponsorship and promotion purposes.

Her YouTube channel, The RuPrint, has 1.3 thousand subscribers with nearly 30 million views as of this writing; even though she has only uploaded four videos. With this channel, she hopes to share the “crazy life” she has been placed in with the public.
