RHOA NeNe Leakes Makes It Clear She Wants Phaedra Parks Back And Porsha Williams Fired

July 2024 · 2 minute read

NeNe Leakes is making changes to her alliances on the Real Housewives of Atlanta. The RHOA star is reportedly ending her dispute with Phaedra Parks while demanding that producers cut ties with her new enemy, Porsha Williams.

The drama started after Williams revealed that Leakes was the one who spilled information about Kandi Burruss’s beef with Dennis McKinley.  After the news of the betrayal broke, Leakes fired back on Instagram and slammed her RHOA co-star for stabbing her in the back.

Have u ever had a close friend that was setting you up for the kill? The gag is, you know it tho! They however wants EVERYBODY to think they are nice, pretty and supportive! but really they r insecure, not that pretty, not talented and certainly not nice! Their day is coming

— NeNe Leakes (@NeNeLeakes) December 18, 2018

“When you ride or die chick!!!! Hey lil sis, I got your back! Lis sis: Yeah ok! Then throws big sis under the bus!” Leakes wrote. “What the F—k.”

Williams immediately answered and claims that clever editing made it seem like she gave Leakes up. Williams then assured Leakes that she is not the one who threw her under the bus.

An inside source, meanwhile, told Us Magazine that Williams was very upset by Leakes’ post on social media. To make things worse, Leakes has been giving Williams the cold shoulder in an attempt to isolate her.

This is one reason why Williams was not invited to Leakes’ recent birth bash.

As if that is not enough, Leakes has also made amends with Parks, who was fired after her drama with Burruss and her husband, Todd Tucker.

In fact, Parks was invited to Leakes’ birthday party last weekend and even took time to pose for some photos with the RHOA star.

Sources claim that Leakes is now pushing for Parks to return to the show. She also reportedly wants to see Williams get fired, though it is not clear if producers will give in to her demands.

One source close to the situation says that Williams wants to make amends with Leakes, but she is unwilling to play nice.

Unless Leakes changes her mind and gives Williams another shot, their feud is unlikely to end anytime soon.

Nene Leakes has not commented on the rumors surrounding her feud with Williams.
