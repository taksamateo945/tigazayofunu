Gabrielle Union flaunts her toned abs in a bikini with her husband Dwayne Wade in Spain

Gabrielle Union flaunted her incredibly fit physique in an assortment of photos she shared earlier on Friday as she posed with her husband, Dwayne Wade, on a boat. 

The actress, 49, and NBA sports star, 40, have been enjoying time under the sun in Spain for their annual summer getaway, or also known as the Wade World Tour. 

The She’s All That star was all smiles as she captured a few memorable snaps she uploaded to her 20.6 million followers on Instagram. 

Happy: Gabrielle Union, 49, and her husband Dwayne Wade, 40, posed in a few vacation photos during their summer getaway 

The stunning actress was decked out in colorful Valentino to fully immerse herself in the summer trip. 

She wore a vibrant orange two-piece swimsuit, with the bikini top tied in place and secured with string. 

When she wasn’t sunbathing, Gabrielle added a Valentino Round Rain Print Charmeuse top, which is priced at $2,300, according to the brand’s website. 

The 10 Things I Hate About You star also added a cotton and silk headband made of the same colorful print, which is marked at $570.  

A day under the sun: The two stars posed in a strew of adorable vacation photos as they relaxed on a boat near the Majorca Island in Spain 

Cute: The actress’s husband and NBA star was seen posing with a professional camera with his wife sending a smile towards him 

In a few of the snaps, Dwayne could be seeing donning reflective sunglasses along with a white, printed T-shirt as he lounged under the bright sun. 

He did an outfit change later on and was seen wearing a black tank top along with pink and blue swim shorts.  

Another photo showed the couple snuggling close on the boat, with the sports star planting a kiss on Gabrielle’s shoulder. 

During the boat day, the talented actress shared a short video onto her Instagram story where she flaunted her summer body and gave a glimpse of the stunning scenery surrounding her.  

Adorable: The happy couple were pictured cozying up on the boat as the sun shone high in the sky

Dance time: Gabrielle uploaded a video onto her Instagram story showing off a few dance moves as she filmed the surrounding scenery off the island in Spain

Each year, the actress and her family take time to enjoy the summer weather by going on a lavish vacation together before the season changes. 

In the Instagram story video, the hard-working mother was lip-syncing to a song playing in the background, and she appeared to be having the time of her life. 

Living her best life: Gabrielle was seen sending a smile to the camera as she danced and lip-synced to a song as she filmed herself on the boat

Although Gabrielle and Dwayne are holding onto summer before fall comes, the two are preparing for the release of their latest children’s book. 

The talented duo wrote the story, Shady Baby Feels: A First Look At Emotions, which will be available  this month on August 23. They collaborated on their first Shady Baby book last year in 2021. 

According to an official synopsis, the book was written to help young children, ‘Learn about feelings and emotions.’ 

The Bring It On star has released a children’s book in the past with, Welcome To The Party, in 2020. She also wrote a memoir titled, We’re Going To Need More Wine, in 2017, followed by a second memoir called, You Got Anything Stronger?: Stories, in 2021.
